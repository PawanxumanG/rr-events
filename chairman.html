RR INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE

Chairman’s Message

Shri R. R. Pardeshi
Chairman, RR International College

Shri R. R. Pardeshi is a highly eminent educationist who has played a pivotal role in strengthening the Secondary, Higher Secondary (SSC–HSC) and Higher Education system of Maharashtra. He has served in top leadership positions including Director of Higher Education (Nagpur Region), Joint Director of Higher Education (Mumbai & Nagpur), and Chairman of the SSC & HSC Board (Nashik Region). His work significantly improved academic standards, examination systems, and educational governance across the state.

He has also worked as Deputy Director of Education (Mumbai & Nagpur) and represented the Government of Maharashtra on prestigious university bodies such as Management Councils, Senates, Academic Councils, Boards of Examination, and Finance Committees of Mumbai, SNDT, and Nagpur Universities. Honored four times by the Honorable Governor of Maharashtra, he has represented the state at international education conferences and academic visits to Japan, South Korea, Singapore, Thailand, and Dubai. His visionary leadership continues to guide RR International College toward academic excellence and disciplined higher education.
